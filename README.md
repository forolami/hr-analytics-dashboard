# HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

## Overview
An interactive HR Analytics dashboard built in Power BI providing 
full workforce insights to support HR decision-making and strategic 
people management.

## Dashboard Highlights
- **Total Employees:** 1,470 across all departments
- **Gender Split:** 60% Male (882) | 40% Female (588)
- **Active Employees:** 1,353 (92%) currently on service
- **Retrenched:** 117 employees (8%)
- **Due for Promotion:** 72 employees (4.9%)
- **Not Due for Promotion:** 1,398 employees (95.1%)

## Visuals Built
- KPI Cards — Total headcount, gender distribution, 
  promotion status, retrenchment rate
- Service Year horizontal bar chart — tenure distribution 
  across workforce
- Job Levels bar chart — employee distribution across 
  5 job levels
- Distance Status donut chart — Very Close, Close, Very Far 
  employee breakdown
- Multi-page navigation — Home, Action, and Detail views

## Business Insights
- 8% retrenchment rate signals workforce restructuring activity 
  requiring HR attention
- Level 1 and Level 2 employees form the largest workforce 
  segments — succession planning opportunity
- 63.95% of employees live very far from office — 
  remote work policy consideration recommended
- Only 4.9% of workforce is due for promotion — 
  retention risk if career progression is not addressed

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI (DAX, Data Modelling, Dashboard Design)
- HR Analytics Dataset
